1. Objectives
1.1 Business Objective
Bellabeat seeks to uncover new avenues for business expansion by thoroughly analysing data from its current user base who engage with the company's smart devices. The primary aim is to identify significant trends and patterns within the data, which will inform strategic recommendations to propel the company's growth.
